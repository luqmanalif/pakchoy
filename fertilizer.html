how to make fertilizer

we will only be using organic ingredients that can easily be obtained at home. The ingredients are actually scraps and waste from our everyday cooking.

what u will need:

  • plastic bottle or any kind of container
  • rice water that u use to wash the rice
  • old vegetables
  • egg shells
  • some soil

procedure

  1. cut the veggies into small chunks
  2. crush the eggshells, preferably into powder
  3. put all of the ingredients in the container
  4. mix and let it sit for a week before using
